Speaker Lineup

Our speakers for this year's Tech Week at a Great Location were carefully selected to create an extraordinary experience. Each are highly qualified with various background in the tech industry. Hopefully your schedule doesn't conflict with any of them; you won't want to miss their very long presentations!

Cassandra Reyes

Dr. Cassandra Reyes stands as a titan in the realm of technology. Armed with a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Stanford University, Dr. Reyes blazed a trail through the tech landscape, challenging conventions and redefining industry standards. Her journey began with a relentless thirst for knowledge and a rebellious spirit that refused to accept the status quo. Dr. Cassandra Reyes is a beacon of light guiding us toward a future limited only by our imagination

Jason Nguyen

Jason Nguyen is a towering figure in the field of computer engineering, renowned for his visionary leadership and relentless pursuit of innovation. His passion for understanding the intricacies of hardware and software alike set him on a trajectory towards greatness from the very beginning. Jason captivates audiences with his dynamic presence and thought-provoking insights.

Emily Park

Emily Park is a professional web developer with a career marked by a consistent commitment to excellence. With a degree in Computer Science and a specialization in web development, Emily embarked on her journey into the tech world with determination and a passion for creating beautiful, functional websites. Through her dedication and unwavering commitment, she has helped shape the digital landscape, leaving behind a legacy of elegance and innovation that will endure for years to come.
